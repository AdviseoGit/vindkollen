# Vindkollen Weekly Maintenance Report - 2026-06-08

## 1. Content Performance Summary

Due to very low organic search traffic, it's difficult to label content as "top-performing" or "underperforming" in a traditional sense. The primary pages receiving impressions are the **homepage (`/`)** and the **Ersättningskalkylator (`/kalkylator`)**.

The existing blog content, while well-focused on the financial niche for landowners, is currently receiving negligible organic traffic. This suggests that while the quality may be good, the topics are not currently aligned with high-volume search queries in Sweden.
